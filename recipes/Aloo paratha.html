ALOO PARATHA

Description

Aloo Paratha is a popular North Indian stuffed flatbread made with whole wheat flour and a spicy mashed potato filling.
It is typically served with butter, yogurt, or pickles and is a hearty and delicious breakfast or meal option.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 1 teaspoon oil or ghee

For the filling

  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh coriander leaves, chopped

For Cooking:

  • Ghee or butter for roasting

STEPS

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a bowl, mix whole wheat flour, salt, and oil. Gradually add water and knead into a soft dough.
    Cover and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Filling: In a separate bowl, combine mashed potatoes, green chili, ginger, cumin seeds, red chili powder,
    garam masala, coriander powder, amchur, salt, and coriander leaves. Mix well.
  3. Stuff the Paratha: Take a small portion of the dough and roll it into a small circle. Place a spoonful of potato filling in the center,
    seal the edges, and roll it out gently into a round paratha.
  4. Cook the Paratha: Heat a tawa (griddle) on medium heat. Place the rolled paratha on the tawa and cook for a minute.
    Flip, apply ghee, and cook until golden brown spots appear. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Serve Hot: Enjoy the Aloo Paratha with butter, yogurt, or pickles for a delicious meal.
