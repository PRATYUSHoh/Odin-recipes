Pav Bhaji

Description

Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food dish consisting of a spicy mashed vegetable curry (bhaji) served with buttered and toasted bread rolls (pav).
Originating from Mumbai, this delicious and flavorful dish is a favorite among food lovers and is often enjoyed as a snack or a full meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes
  • boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup cauliflower
  • finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup capsicum, finely chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon pav bhaji masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 chopped lemon cut into wedges
  • 4 pav (bread rolls)
  • Extra butter for toasting pav

STEPS

  1. Heat oil and butter in a large pan on medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.Then add chopped onions and sautÃ© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears
  4. Stir in chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
  5. Add chopped capsicum, cauliflower, and green peas. SautÃ© for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add boiled and mashed potatoes along with red chili powder,turmeric powder, coriander powder, and pav bhaji masala.
  7. Pour in a cup of water and mash everything together using a potato masher until well combined.
  8. Let the mixture simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add salt to taste
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and squeeze lemon juice for extra flavor.
  10. Heat a pan with butter, slice the pav rolls in half, and toast them until golden brown.
  11. Serve the hot bhaji with buttered pav, chopped onions, and lemon wedges.
